Rocket Science - Idiom Package  

Rocket Science is for anyone. No skill is required for Rocket Science. Its box resembles a nostalgic time when games were fun and didn’t require much thought. It’s well-loved with rough spots from many uses throughout generations. The box’s design brings the quirky look of children’s game boxes and bright colourful colours that have a feeling of joy and excitement. The caricature of the young man also adds a personal touch that this is a recreation of a typical 60s game box. The use of minimal colours and patterns brings into the methods that printing in that time may have influenced the mid-century modern look we know and love.
